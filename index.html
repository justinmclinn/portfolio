United States Marine Corps

MSgt Justin N. McLinn

Senior Software Engineer

Marine Corps Software Factory


Master Sergeant McLinn currently serves as a Senior Software Engineer for the Marine Corps Software Factory.

Born in Grand Junction Colorado, and raised in North Branch Minnesota, Master Sergeant McLinn attended recruit training at Marine Corps Recruit Depot Sand Diego, CA in December 2008.

Throughout his career Master Sergeant McLinn has been assigned to numerous duties to include: Data System Specialist, Data System Specialist Noncommissioned Officer (NCO) in Charge; Basic Recruiter, Assistant Staff Noncommissioned (SNCO) in Charge; Communications Chief, Platoon Sergeant, Helpdesk SNCO in Charge; Assistant Cybersecurity Chief, Information System Security Manager, Application Developer and Senior Software Engineer.

He has deployed with Regimental Combat Team 8 in support of Operation Enduring Freedom. He assisted in the setup of network and systems architectures on the USS Iwo Jima in support of New York City Fleet Week in 2010, and again in 2012 on the USS Wasp.

During his career he has received the following formal military training: Career Course and Advanced Course at the SNCO Academy Camp Lejeune, NC; Basic Recruiter Course at Marine Corps Recruit Depot San Diego, CA; Data Systems Specialist Course, Data Systems Chief Course at Marine Corps Communication-Electronics School Twentynine Palms California; Intro to Satellite Communications and Secure Mobile Anti-Jam Reliable Tactical Terminal (SMART-T) at Fort Gordon, GA; Tactical Data Network Data Distribution System, CompTIA Network+ and CompTIA Security+ at Communications Training Center II Camp Lejeune, NC; and Computer Systems Programming Course at Keesler Air Force Base Biloxi, MS.

His personal awards include the Navy and Marine Corps Achievement Medal with 1 Gold Star.
